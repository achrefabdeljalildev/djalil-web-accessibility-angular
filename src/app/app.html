Accessibility Widget Demo

This is a demo page showcasing the Angular Accessibility Widget. Click the blue button in the bottom-right corner to open the accessibility settings panel.

Features

The widget provides various accessibility options including high contrast mode, dark mode, readable fonts, reading mask, text magnifier, and more.
